Our Story

A space built for beauty, comfort & confidence

Oksana Salon is a contemporary beauty space in the heart of Norwich, offering expert hair styling, colouring, nail treatments, brows, lashes, and a full range of modern beauty services.

Every appointment is a personalised experience — thoughtful, attentive, and designed to make you look and feel your absolute best. We combine technical mastery with a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
