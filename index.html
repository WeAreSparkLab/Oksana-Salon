Our Story

A space built for beauty, comfort & confidence

Oksana Salon is a well-loved beauty destination on Catton Grove Road, known especially for its nail art and attention to detail — alongside expert hair styling, colouring, hair botox, lash extensions, lip fillers and a full range of beauty treatments.

Our team speaks English, Russian, Lithuanian, Latvian and Polish, so every client feels understood and at ease. With free parking right outside, popping in is easy too.
